Privacy Policy – Resumex

1. Introduction
Resumex respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the Resumex application.

2. Account & Authentication Information
When you sign up or log in, we collect authentication-related information such as your email address and user ID. This data is used only for account identification and login purposes.

3. Resume Data Storage
Resume content including experience, education, skills, and summaries is NOT stored on our servers or in Supabase. All resume data remains locally on your device and is only processed temporarily when AI features are used.

4. AI Usage
Resumex uses AI to help generate and improve resume content. Your data is processed temporarily to generate suggestions and is never used to train AI models.

5. Automatically Collected Information
We may collect limited technical data such as device type, app usage statistics, and crash reports to improve performance and stability.

6. Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data may only be disclosed if required by law or to protect users and the application.

7. Data Security
We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no digital storage method is 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy
Resumex is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app.
